About Me

Hi, I'm Matt, a PhD candidate at the University of California, Davis. I study plant biology, with a focus on how somatic mutation effects clonally propagated plants.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me reading, exploring the outdoors, or tinkering with side projects. I'm always open to new opportunities and collaborations.

Walnut Mutation Accumulation

Scripts and analyses investigating somatic mutation accumulation in clonally propagated walnut plants, supporting research into how plants acquire mutations over vegetative generations. The manuscript this data supports is currently available as a preprint on bioRxiv and in review.
